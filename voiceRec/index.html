Speak
           Eazy

A speech accessibility software empowering individuals with hearing impairments and mutism.It seamlessly transcribes spoken language into text and vice versa and also provides real-time sign language interpretation.


Speakeazy bridges communication barriers, enhancing inclusivity and enabling effective communication for all. Speakeazy also has translators and voice search products to help in communication and research as well.

Problem :

Individuals with co-occurring mutism and hearing impairments face significant challenges in communication, leading to restricted social interactions, diminished access to healthcare, education, and employment opportunities, and reduced overall well-being. The lack of comprehensive understanding and support for this population within the healthcare system hampers their ability to receive appropriate care and fully engage in society. It is crucial to implement targeted interventions, inclusive healthcare policies, and accessible communication strategies to address their specific needs and promote equitable healthcare, education, and social inclusion globally.

Speakeazy,

is here to aid and assist individuals with such problems.

Our Solution

Introducing our products to aid in breaking the communication barrier between individuals experiencing mutism and hearing impairment.

  • Sign Language Interpreter
  • Sign Language Transcriber
  • Speech-To-Text recognition
  • Text-To-Speech recognition
  • Voice search
  • Language Translator
